About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Oslo, studying tumor heterogeneity in breast cancer through the reconstruction and comparison of single sample regulatory networks. During my PhD at Oregon State University, I studied the interplay between the gut microbiome and diseases (especially cancer and metabolic disease) of the host. Through the use of systems biology and network biology, I identified key factors (genes, microbiota, etc.) that are vital in the response to a treatment.
